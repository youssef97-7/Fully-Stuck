The Great Gatsby

F. Scott Fitzgerald

Classic Literature American 1920s 4.8  (2.4k reviews)

Description

Set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, near New York City, the novel depicts first-person narrator Nick Carraway's interactions with mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and Gatsby's obsession to reunite with his former lover, Daisy Buchanan.

A classic of American literature, "The Great Gatsby" explores themes of decadence, idealism, resistance to change, social upheaval, and excess, creating a cautionary tale regarding the American Dream.
